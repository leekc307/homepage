Short Bio

Zheng Wen is currently a senior research scientist at Big Data Experience Lab, Adobe Research. His current research focuses on machine learning, operations research, and big data.

Before joining Adobe Research, he was a research scientist in Advertising Science Team, Yahoo Labs. Prior to that, he received a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University, under the supervision of Prof. Benjamin Van Roy. He also holds a Ph.D. minor in Business/Finance from Stanford Graduate School of Business. Prior to that, he received a M.S. in Electrical Engineering from Washington State University, and a B.E. in Electrical Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology. He is a member of INFORMS and IEEE.
