Short Bio

Kuang-chih Lee is a research director who manages the advertising marketplace optimization data science team at Yahoo. His research interests lie at the intersection of online advertising, big data, distributed system, machine learning, data mining, NLP, and computer vision. He has more than 20 research papers published in top conferences (CVPR, NIPS, and KDD) and journals (PAMI, and CVIU). As reported by Google Scholar, there are more 2000 citations to his publications. Prior to joining Yahoo Labs, Kuang-chih Lee was the principal scientist at Turn Inc. Before that he held various research and development leadership positions at Flashfoto, DigitalPersona, and like.com. like.com was acquired by google in year 2010. Kuang-chih Lee received CS PhD degree in UIUC in 2005.
